IN THE HIGH COURT OF JUSTICE
QUEEN'S BENCH DIVISION
JOHN DOE, Claimant
vs.
XYZ CORPORATION, Defendant
CLAIM FORM - TRADEMARK INFRINGEMENT
The Claimant, John Doe, by his solicitors, hereby issues this Claim
Form for Trademark Infringement against the Defendant XYZ
Corporation, and makes the following allegations:
1. The Claimant is a resident of the United Kingdom and is the
registered proprietor of the trademark "ABC."
2. The Defendant XYZ Corporation is a company registered in England
and Wales, with its registered office located in London.
3. The Claimant brings this action against the Defendant for trademark
infringement under the Trade Marks Act 1994 and related common law
claims.
